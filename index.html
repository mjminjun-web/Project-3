iHeart Radio

340 Townsend Street

Locate

iHeartRadio has a presence in San Francisco because it’s one of the largest and most influential media markets in the United States, especially for tech, culture and entertainment. It encompasses a wide array of contemporary radio stations. Being in the Bay Area gives the company access to a huge, diverse audience and opportunities to partner with local events, artists and advertisers.

  

Scale

650 Townsend Street

Locate

Scale AI’s San Francisco office is central to its mission of accelerating AI development by providing high-quality data and machine learning solutions. With around 800 employees and growing, the office drives innovation and collaboration across engineering, product, and customer teams. Being in San Francisco connects Scale to top tech talent and partners, supporting clients from major tech firms to government agencies. Scale’s presence highlights its leadership in the global AI industry.

  

Pylon

340 Townsend Street

Locate

Pylon’s San Francisco headquarters is the hub for its modern B2B customer support platform, unifying tools for support, success, and feedback. Founded by experienced engineers, Pylon has quickly grown to over 250 customers and a team of 14, with plans to expand further. Its in-person office culture and Bay Area location help attract top talent and foster rapid innovation, making Pylon a rising star in enterprise software.

  

Samsara

1 De Haro Street

Locate

Samsara is a fleet management company that specializes in providing physical operations technology. They serve all major industries in some form through their share of of IoT (Internet of Things). Through cloud-based software and AI powered analytics, they help with anthropological research, construction, utilities and running of public sector organizations.
